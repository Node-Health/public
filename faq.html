Currently, our app does not provide specific health or medical recommendations. We place great importance on accuracy and reliability and are cautious due to the sensitive nature of health-related advice.

However, we do provide basic information about what can be done to keep your values within the normal range. Please note that this consists of publicly available information and is not tailored to your personal situation. It’s also important to understand that all information provided by the app does not constitute medical advice. We always recommend consulting healthcare professionals for personalized medical guidance before taking any specific action.
