            Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)        

       
                       
                                    How exactly is my data stored?                                                    
                   

We store all data directly on your smartphone. It never leaves your smartphone. By deleting the app, you also delete your data. We do not store any data on our servers.

               
           
                       
                                    Can I export my data?                                                    
                   

Yes, you can export your data directly in the app at any time, for example, to keep it for yourself or to transfer it to a new phone.

               
           
                       
                                    How much does it cost to use the app?                                                    
                   

The Node Health App is completely free and available in the iOS App Store.

               
           
                       
                                    Who is the app suitable for?                                                    
                   

The app is ideal for you if you want to proactively manage your health. It is ideal for data-conscious people who not only want to understand their biomarkers but also track them and bring them into the optimal range. The app can be used to prepare for doctor's visits and to conduct a data-driven dialogue with medical professionals.

               
           
                                    
                                    Does the app provide medical recommendations?                                                    
                   

Currently, our app provides no specific health or medical recommendations. We place the highest value on the accuracy and relevance of our services and are very cautious in this area due to the sensitive nature of health-related advice.

                   

However, we do provide you with basic information that shows you what can be done to keep your values within the normal range. Please note that this is publicly accessible information that is not tailored to your individual case. Furthermore, it is important to know that all information provided by the app is not to be understood as medical advice. We always recommend consulting a medical professional for personalized medical advice before taking specific steps.
